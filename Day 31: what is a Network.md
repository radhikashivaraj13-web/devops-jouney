what is a Network?
  A computer network is a collection of interconnected computing devices that communicate with each other over wired or wireless links to share resources,exchange data and access services.
what is a packet?
  A packet is aformatted,small unit of data sent over a network,acting as the fundamental building block of internet communication.
what is difference between LAN and WAN?
  LAN connects devices with small,private area like a home or office,while a WAN covers alarge geographical distances,connecting multiple LAN'S across cities or countries.
What does a router do?
  A router is networking device that connects multiple devices to the internet and to each other ,acting as a central dispatcher that directs the data traffic.
